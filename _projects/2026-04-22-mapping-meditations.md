**Mapping Meditations** are a series of scores exploring spatial awareness.

Inspired by Pauline Olivero's 'Sonic Meditations', these scores invite participants to create, reflect, and share maps for different purposes.

These meditations use the term 'map' loosely, extending beyond the discipline of cartography to include other ways of orienting, ones that are perhaps more embodied and relational.

[The scores are continuously updated as new sessions are hosted.](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1Lf3yz-grjCGxa0JntkSNmYr-oRMCnCUmyvWaepqx8Do/edit?tab=t.0)

_These meditations were first trialed at the [School of Commons in June 2024](https://hackmd.io/@aleesteele/soc-mapping-meditations), and [debuted publicly as a workshop](https://zenodo.org/records/19675537) for the MA in Communication Design at Central Saint Martins, University Arts London._
